     8→---
     9→
    10→## 1. PHILOSOPHY
    11→
    12→### This Is Not a Form. It's a Discovery Conversation.
    13→
    14→The old form asked: "What do you want?" and left it at that. This questionnaire asks the right questions in the right order — so by the end, we know exactly what they need, why they need it, and how to help.
    15→
    16→Every step builds on the last. Their purpose shapes their pain. Their pain shapes the project details. The project details shape the solution. No wasted questions.
    17→
    18→### The Emotional Arc
    19→
    20→```
    21→Step 1: "What do you need?"           → Purpose — pick the service(s)
    22→Step 2: "What's broken?"             → Pain — tailored to their purpose
    23→Step 3: "Tell me more"               → Project details — tailored to their pain
    24→Step 4: "Who are you?"               → Name, email, company (optional)
    25→Step 5: "How much to fix this?"      → Budget — realistic investment
    26→Step 6: "Did we get it right?"       → Full summary confirmation
    27→```
    28→

    31→## 2. QUESTIONNAIRE STRUCTURE
    32→
    33→### Step 1: The Opening
    34→
    35→```
    36→┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
    37→│                                                   │
    38→│         What do you need help with?               │
    39→│                                                   │
    40→│    Pick everything that applies. We'll            │
    41→│    ask the right follow-up questions.             │
    42→│                                                   │
    43→└─────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
    44→```
    45→
    46→**Options (multi-select):**
    47→
    48→| # | Service | Icon | Description (shown below option on select) |
    49→|---|---------|------|---------------------------------------------|
    50→| 1 | Workflow Automation | ⚙️ | Repetitive tasks, data entry, cross-tool syncing |
    51→| 2 | Intelligent Agents | 🤖 | Customer support, sales qualification, operations |
    52→| 3 | Smart Assistants | 🧠 | Calendar, email triage, document processing |
    53→| 4 | Business Intelligence | 📊 | Dashboards, reports, KPI tracking |
    54→| 5 | Web Development | 🌐 | Websites, landing pages, web apps |
    55→| 6 | UI/UX Design | 🎨 | App design, interface design, user experience |
    56→| 7 | Digital Products | 📱 | SaaS tools, mobile apps, proprietary software |
    57→| 8 | Custom Solutions | 🛠️ | Internal tools, portals, something from scratch |
    58→
    59→**Behavior:**
    60→- Multi-select (checkbox cards, no limit)
    61→- Each selected option highlights with `border-gray-900 bg-gray-50`
    62→- A small counter appears: "You picked 3 services"
    63→- If they pick 0 and click Next → error: "Pick at least one"
    64→
    65→**Follow-up micro-text:**
    66→> "Great choices. Let's dig into what's going on."

    69→
    70→### Step 2: "What's broken?"
    71→
    72→**This step changes based on what they selected in Step 1.**
    73→
    74→The pain options are pulled from a service→pain map. If they selected multiple services, all relevant pain options appear (deduplicated). The more services they picked, the more pain options appear — and the more they can select.
    75→
    76→**Selection rules:**
    77→- 1 service selected → up to 2 pain points
    78→- 2 services selected → up to 3 pain points
    79→- 3+ services selected → up to 4 pain points
    80→
    81→#### Service → Pain Map
    82→
    83→**Workflow Automation →**
    84→- Copy-pasting data between tools all day
    85→- Same tasks repeated every week with no end in sight
    86→- Manual approval processes bottlenecking everything
    87→- Errors from human data entry costing me money
    88→- Onboarding new employees takes forever
    89→
    90→**Intelligent Agents →**
    91→- Customers waiting too long for responses
    92→- Losing leads because nobody followed up in time
    93→- Support team buried in repetitive questions
    94→- Inconsistent quality across customer interactions
    95→- Can't scale customer service without hiring more people
    96→
    97→**Smart Assistants →**
    98→- Email overload — important messages getting buried
    99→- Scheduling wars — too many back-and-forths
   100→- Documents piling up with no system to process them
   101→- Admin work eating into billable hours
   102→- Team spending more time organizing than working
   103→
   104→**Business Intelligence →**
   105→- Flying blind — no idea what's actually happening in my business
   106→- Building reports by hand every week/month
   107→- Data scattered across 5 different tools
   108→- Can't spot problems until they're expensive
   109→- No real-time visibility into performance
   110→
   111→**Custom Solutions →**
   112→- Off-the-shelf software doesn't fit how I actually work
   113→- Patching together 4 tools to do what 1 should
   114→- Need something specific that doesn't exist yet
   115→- My current system is held together with duct tape
   116→- Scaling is impossible with what I have now
   117→
   118→#### Always shown (regardless of service selection):
   119→- It's a mix of everything, honestly
   120→- I just need someone who gets it
   121→- [Something else...]
   122→
   123→**Custom option:** [Something else...]
   124→→ Expands text input: "Tell us what's going on"
   125→
   126→**Behavior:**
   127→- Multi-select (checkbox cards, limit varies by service count)
   128→- After selection, personalized follow-up:
   129→
   130→> "That's the kind of stuff we love fixing."

   134→### Step 3: "Tell me more about your project"
   135→
   136→**This step changes based on both their purpose (Step 1) AND their pain (Step 2).**
   137→
   138→The project detail options are a combination of service-specific and pain-specific follow-ups. This gives us the most useful context for scoping.
   139→
   140→#### If they picked Workflow Automation:
   141→- I need to automate data entry between [tool] and [tool]
   142→- I want a system that handles approvals without me chasing people
   143→- I need recurring tasks to run on autopilot
   144→- I want to reduce errors in [specific process]
   145→
   146→#### If they picked Intelligent Agents:
   147→- I need something to handle customer support 24/7
   148→- I want agents to qualify and follow up with leads
   149→- I need an internal operations agent for my team
   150→- I want to automate [specific workflow] with smart decisions
   151→
   152→#### If they picked Smart Assistants:
   153→- I need help managing my email and calendar
   154→- I want document processing automated
   155→- I need an assistant that keeps my team organized
   156→- I want to automate [specific admin task]
   157→
   158→#### If they picked Business Intelligence:
   159→- I need a real-time dashboard for my business
   160→- I want automated reports delivered on a schedule
   161→- I need to connect data from multiple tools into one view
   162→- I want to track [specific KPIs]
   163→
   164→#### If they picked Custom Solutions:
   165→- I need an internal tool built from scratch
   166→- I want a customer-facing portal or app
   167→- I need an API integration between systems
   168→- I want to build [specific thing]
   169→
   170→#### If they picked Web Development:
   171→- My website doesn't represent who I am anymore
   172→- I need a site that actually converts visitors into customers
   173→- My site is slow, broken on mobile, or just ugly
   174→- I need a landing page for a specific campaign or product
   175→- I want to redesign my site without rebuilding from scratch
   176→
   177→#### If they picked UI/UX Design:
   178→- My app looks like it was built in 2010
   179→- Users are confused by my interface — they keep dropping off
   180→- I need a design system that keeps everything consistent
   181→- I want a mobile app that actually feels good to use
   182→- I need wireframes and prototypes before building anything
   183→
   184→#### If they picked Digital Products:
   185→- I want to build a SaaS product and need a technical partner
   186→- I have an idea for an app but don't know where to start
   187→- I need to turn my existing process into a product others can use
   188→- I want to monetize my expertise through a digital tool
   189→- I need help scoping and building an MVP
   190→
   191→#### Always shown (regardless of service selection):
   192→- I'm not 100% sure yet — help me figure it out
   193→- I need the full package — design, build, and deploy
   194→- [I'll explain...]
   195→
   196→**Custom option:** [I'll explain...]
   197→→ Expands text input: "Tell us about your project — what does it look like, what tools do you use, what would make your life easier?"
   198→
   199→**Behavior:**
   200→- Multi-select (checkbox cards, up to 3)
   201→- Custom option always available
   202→- Answers stored as `project_details` array + optional `project_description` text
   203→
   204→**Follow-up micro-text:**
   205→> "That gives us a lot to work with. Perfect."

   222→│    │  Last Name                           │       │
   223→│    └──────────────────────────────────────┘       │
   224→│    ┌──────────────────────────────────────┐       │
   225→│    │  Email *                             │       │
   226→│    └──────────────────────────────────────┘       │
   227→│    ┌──────────────────────────────────────┐       │
   228→│    │  Phone                               │       │

   230→│    ┌──────────────────────────────────────┐       │
   231→│    │  Company Name (optional)             │       │
   232→│    └──────────────────────────────────────┘       │
   233→│                                                   │
   234→│    How do you prefer we reach you?                │

   239→│    [ Morning ] [ Afternoon ] [ Evening ] [ Any ]  │
   240→│                                                   │
   241→│    Anything else we should know?                  │
   242→│    ┌──────────────────────────────────────┐       │
   243→│    │                                     │       │

   245→│                                                   │
   246→└─────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

   248→
   249→**Fields:**
   250→
   251→| Field | Type | Required |
   252→|-------|------|----------|
   253→| First Name | text | ✅ |
   254→| Last Name | text | ❌ |
   255→| Email | email | ✅ |
   256→| Phone | tel | ❌ |
   257→| Company Name | text | ❌ |
   258→| Preferred contact method | checkboxes (Email / Phone / WhatsApp) | ✅ (at least one) |
   259→| Best time to reach you | radio (Morning / Afternoon / Evening / Anytime) | ❌ |
   260→| Anything else? | textarea | ❌ |
   261→
   262→**Behavior:**
   263→- Email is always required (even if they don't check "Email" as preference — it's added automatically)
   264→- If they check Phone → phone field appears with validation
   265→- If they check WhatsApp → WhatsApp field appears with validation
   266→- Company field is always visible but marked optional
   267→- Contact preference stored as array, individual fields stored separately
   268→
   269→**Follow-up micro-text:**
   270→> "Nice to meet you, [first name]."

   273→
   274→### Step 5: "How much to solve this?"
   275→
   276→**Question:** How much are you willing to spend to solve this problem realistically?
   277→
   278→**Options:**
   279→- Under $1,000 — I need something simple
   280→- $1,000 – $5,000 — I want a real solution
   281→- $5,000 – $10,000 — I'm ready to invest properly
   282→- $10,000 – $15,000 — let's do this right
   283→- $15,000+ — I want the best, not the cheapest
   284→- I don't know yet — help me scope it first
   285→- Let's discuss — it depends on what you propose
   286→
   287→**Behavior:**
   288→- Single select (radio-style cards)
   289→- Answer stored as `budget_willingness` field
   290→- Follow-up micro-text (varies):
   291→  - Under $1,000: "We can work with that. Simple doesn't mean ineffective."
   292→  - $1,000 – $5,000: "Solid range. We'll maximize every dollar."
   293→  - $5,000 – $10,000: "Now we're building something real."
   294→  - $10,000 – $15,000: "That budget unlocks serious systems."
   295→  - $15,000+: "World-class budget. Let's build something incredible."
   296→  - Don't know: "No problem. We'll scope it first so you know exactly what you're paying for."
   297→  - Let's discuss: "Smart. The right solution is worth more than a round number."
   298→

   313→>  [Service 1], [Service 2]                          │
   314→│                                                   │
   315→│  WHAT'S BROKEN                                    │
   316→>  [Pain 1], [Pain 2]                                │
   317→│                                                   │
   318→│  YOUR PROJECT                                      │
   319→>  [Project detail 1], [Project detail 2]           │
   320→│  [Custom project description if provided]          │
   321→│                                                   │
   322→│  WHO YOU ARE                                       │
   323→│  Name: [First] [Last]                              │
   324→│  Email: [email]                                    │
   325→│  Phone: [phone] (if provided)                      │
   326→│  Company: [company] (if provided)                  │
   327→│                                                   │
   328→│  HOW TO REACH YOU                                  │
   329→│  Preferred: [contact methods]                      │
   330→│  Best time: [best time]                            │
   331→│                                                   │
   332→│  YOUR INVESTMENT                                   │
   333→>  [budget willingness]                              │
   334→│                                                   │
   335→│  ──────────────────────────────────────────────  │
   336→│                                                   │
   337→│  Looks right?                                     │
   338→│                                                   │
   339→│  [ Yeah, send it → ]     [ ← Go back ]            │
   340→│                                                   │
   341→└─────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
   342→```
   343→
   344→**Each section has a clickable "Edit" link** that jumps back to that step.
   345→
   346→**Design:**
   347→- Background: White with circuitry
   348→- Summary card: `bg-white border border-gray-200 rounded-2xl p-8`
   349→- Sections animate in with stagger (0.1s delay)
   350→- "Go back" on each section
   351→- CTA: "Yeah, send it →"

   440→### Backgrounds

   605→## 5. DATABASE SCHEMA
   606→
   607→```sql
   608→CREATE TABLE onboarding_submissions (
   609→  id UUID DEFAULT gen_random_uuid() PRIMARY KEY,
   610→
   611→  -- Identity
   612→  first_name TEXT NOT NULL,
   613→  last_name TEXT,
   614→  email TEXT NOT NULL,
   615→  phone TEXT,
   616→  company TEXT,
   617→
   618→  -- Purpose
   619→  services_needed TEXT[] NOT NULL DEFAULT '{}',
   620→
   621→  -- Pain
   622→  pain_points TEXT[] NOT NULL DEFAULT '{}',
   623→  pain_description TEXT,
   624→
   625→  -- Project
   626→  project_details TEXT[] NOT NULL DEFAULT '{}',
   627→  project_description TEXT,
   628→
   629→  -- Contact preferences
   630→  contact_preferences TEXT[] NOT NULL DEFAULT '{}',
   631→  best_time TEXT CHECK (best_time IN ('morning', 'afternoon', 'evening', 'anytime')),
   632→
   633→  -- Investment
   634→  budget_willingness TEXT NOT NULL,
   635→
   636→  -- Additional
   637→  additional_notes TEXT,
   638→
   639→  -- Metadata
   640→  completed_at TIMESTAMPTZ DEFAULT NOW(),
   641→  created_at TIMESTAMPTZ DEFAULT NOW(),
   642→
   643→  -- CRM
   644→  status TEXT DEFAULT 'new' CHECK (status IN ('new', 'contacted', 'qualified', 'proposal', 'won', 'lost')),
   645→  summary TEXT,
   646→  tags TEXT[] DEFAULT '{}'
   647→);
   648→
   649→CREATE INDEX idx_onboarding_email ON onboarding_submissions(email);
   650→CREATE INDEX idx_onboarding_status ON onboarding_submissions(status);
   651→CREATE INDEX idx_onboarding_created ON onboarding_submissions(created_at DESC);
   652→
   653→CREATE TABLE onboarding_responses (
   654→  id UUID DEFAULT gen_random_uuid() PRIMARY KEY,
   655→  submission_id UUID REFERENCES onboarding_submissions(id) ON DELETE CASCADE,
   656→  step_number INT NOT NULL,
   657→  step_key TEXT NOT NULL,
   658→  question TEXT NOT NULL,
   659→  answer TEXT NOT NULL,
   660→  answer_type TEXT CHECK (answer_type IN ('selection', 'custom', 'multiple', 'text')),
   661→  created_at TIMESTAMPTZ DEFAULT NOW()
   662→);
   663→
   664→CREATE INDEX idx_responses_submission ON onboarding_responses(submission_id);
   665→
   666→ALTER TABLE onboarding_submissions ENABLE ROW LEVEL SECURITY;
   667→ALTER TABLE onboarding_responses ENABLE ROW LEVEL SECURITY;
   668→
   669→CREATE POLICY "Allow anonymous inserts" ON onboarding_submissions
   670→  FOR INSERT WITH CHECK (true);
   671→CREATE POLICY "Allow anonymous inserts" ON onboarding_responses
   672→  FOR INSERT WITH CHECK (true);
   673→CREATE POLICY "Allow authenticated reads" ON onboarding_submissions
   674→  FOR SELECT USING (auth.role() = 'authenticated');
   675→CREATE POLICY "Allow authenticated reads" ON onboarding_responses
   676→  FOR SELECT USING (auth.role() = 'authenticated');
   677→```
